Jaz Belvedere hotel

This hotel is considered one of the most luxurious hotels in Sharm El-Sheikh and has been designed with touches of traditional Arabic-Moroccan design

rooms

Air conditioning and heating ensure a comfortable indoor climate in the rooms. Most rooms have a private balcony or terrace. Separate bedrooms are another benefit of booking as a convenience offer. A safe and minibar are also available

Activities

Activities can be enjoyed at this resort without additional cost.

  • Swimming with colorful fish
  • Enjoy a glass boat trip
  • Diving with instructors or snorkeling
  • Shopping in the streets of Naama Bay
  • Sailing boats
  • Water skiing
